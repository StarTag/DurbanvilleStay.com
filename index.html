Description

Durbanville Stay is a charming apartment booked on a bed and breakfast basis. This unit is located on the owner's property, which is situated in Durbanville, Cape Town. It is ideal for 4 guests looking for a comfortable stopover, close to a variety of attractions. This duplex has its own entrance and comprises 2 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms. The main bedroom, featuring with a queen-size bed an an en-suite bathroom, is located downstairs. The second room is upstairs and contains 2 single beds, with access to a separate bathroom and a lounge with a TV. This apartment also features a kitchenette that is equipped with a bar fridge, a microwave, a kettle and a toaster. Free Wi-Fi Internet access is also provided. Outside, there is secure, off-street parking for 2 vehicles. Durbanville Stay is located within a 5 km of wine farms, shopping malls and a golf course. Durbanville Race Course, as well as Durbanville Nature Reserve, is a short driving distance away from the apartment.
